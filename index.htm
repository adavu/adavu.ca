About Me

Hello, I'm Ada, a student at the University of Waterloo studying Computing and Financial Management. In high school, I took both computer science and accounting and enjoyed both subjects, for the most part. Thus, why I am in the program that I am in.


During my years in high school, I took part in side projects and extracurriculars that dealt with my program. This website being one of them. I started this project for fun to learn HTML and CSS, I had no thoughts on creating a website but on my journey I decided why not. I created some mobile game apps in Swift for iOS which I have showcased below. I also participated in Google Code-In which is a programming competition directed for high school students to be exposed to open source. Aside from computer projects, I also took part in finance extracurriculars. I went to a Women in Capital Markets conference where they expose female high school students about careers in STEM and Business.


At the moment, I have no direct route of what I want to do. During university, I hope to experiment more through co-op and other extracurriculars.
